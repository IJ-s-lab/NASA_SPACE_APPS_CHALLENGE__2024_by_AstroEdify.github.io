About The Inner Planets


Mercury

Mercury is the smallest planet in our solar system and is known for its short years, long days, extreme temperatures, and weird sunsets. It is only slightly larger than Earth's moon.











Venus

Venus is the second planet from the Sun, and Earth's closest planetary neighbor.













Earth

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only astronomical object known to harbor life.













Mars

Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun and the second smallest planet in the solar system, after Mercury.
